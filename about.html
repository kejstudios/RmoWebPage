About Retired Mafia of Ohio

More than just a name — we’re a group of car enthusiasts dedicated to helping our community.

Who We Are

Retired Mafia of Ohio began as a small group of friends with a shared passion for cars, community, and helping others. What started as weekend meetups and late-night cruises has grown into a volunteer organization that supports motorists across the state.

Whether it’s assisting with a flat tire, supporting a local car meet, or even helping organize the meet, our mission is simple: bring people together and make the road and car meets a better place.

Our Mission

We believe that the automotive community thrives on respect, safety, and support. Retired Mafia of Ohio stands for unity among car enthusiasts — from imports to muscle, classics to modern rides. Every member carries the same goal: lend a hand, share knowledge, and give back to the community that drives us.

Meet the Crew

Our team is made up of everyday people — mechanics, drivers, hobbyists, and car lovers from all walks of life. Each member contributes their time, tools, and talent to keep others rolling safely and to ensure every car event we support runs smoothly.
