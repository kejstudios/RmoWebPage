About Us

Retired Mafia of Ohio is a volunteer group dedicated to helping motorists in need and supporting local car meets across the state. Our members are car enthusiasts and community-minded individuals who believe in giving back through on-the-road assistance and event support.

Our Services

  • Roadside Assistance: Jump starts, tire changes, and emergency help for stranded drivers.
  • Car Meet Support: Setup, coordination, and safety assistance for local automotive events.
  • Community Outreach: Volunteer projects that strengthen the car scene as a whole.

Contact Us

Want to join our volunteer crew or request assistance? Reach out today — we’re always happy to help our fellow drivers on the road.
